Team and partners

The EnzymeML project is a global, cross-disciplinary initiative that is propelled by collaboration and interconnectedness among the international community, resulting in a continuously expanding EnzymeML community. To ensure optimal organization, the EnzymeML Project Core Team assumes responsibility for the technical advancement of the EnzymeML standard and specifications, as well as overseeing the administration of community resources.
