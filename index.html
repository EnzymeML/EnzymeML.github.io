What is EnzymeML?

EnzymeML is a freely accessible, open standard XML-based format, imbued with the principles of data fairness and transparency. Its primary purpose is to facilitate the storage and transfer of enzyme kinetics data across a diverse range of electronic laboratory notebooks, software tools, and databases.
