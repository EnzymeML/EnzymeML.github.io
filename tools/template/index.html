EnzymeML Spreadsheet

If writing XML or programming in Python is not your forte, have no fear. Competency in these areas is not a requirement for our users. To simplify the process, we offer a user-friendly spreadsheet template for reporting experiments that can easily be transformed into EnzymeML format. Simply download the template, commence your reporting, and upon completion, upload the spreadsheet to receive a well-formed EnzymeML document ready for dissemination into the EnzymeML toolbox.
